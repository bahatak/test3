Here's an Email for You.

This is a to-standards email template for you to rework, brand, and spruce up to your heart's delight. The header and footer respond to and fill in the full width of the viewport, while their contents remain centered. Included are several of the usual blocks that appear in a typical marketing email. Simply use the blocks you need and delete the rest. Happy emailing!
